Server Rules & Regulations

Main Rules Gang & Guild Rules Punishment System

All players are expected to follow these rules at all times. Failure to comply will result in warnings, bans, or permanent removal from the server.

Server Main Rules

Server Main Rules

1

Recording is Mandatory

You must record or stream whenever you're online. If your PC can handle it, stream live instead of just recording. Helps with content and serves as evidence if needed.

2

No Combat Logging

Do not log out during combat or when being chased. You must stay in the game and face the outcome.

3

No Cheating / Hacks

No hacked clients, x-ray, auto-clickers, or any other cheats. ⚠️ Instant permanent ban if caught.

4

No Duping / Exploiting

Item duplication or using glitches/exploits is strictly prohibited. Play fair—no shortcuts.

5

Equal Rules for All

All rules apply equally to everyone—no exceptions for gender, status, or seniority.

6

No Unnecessary PvP or Fights

PvP is only allowed when both sides agree or during events. Random attacks or harassment will not be tolerated.

7

No Racism, Hate Speech, or Abusive Language

This includes racist slurs, hate comments, and parental insults. Be respectful or be removed.

8

Respect Villagers

Don't kill or harm villagers unless it's part of gameplay (e.g. raids, trading setup).

9

No Griefing

Do not destroy, steal, or mess with another player's property. Respect others' space and builds.

10

Use In-Game Voice Chat Only

Do not use Discord voice during gameplay. Stay immersive—all voice chat must happen in-game.

11

Voice Changer? Inform Admins First

If you want to use a voice changer, you must inform the admins first. Surprise use without approval is not allowed.

Gang & Guild Rules

Gang & Guild Rules

1

Minimum Members

A gang/guild must have between 4-8 active members to be officially recognized. Smaller groups are considered individuals.

2

War Declaration

You must have a valid roleplay reason to declare war (territory dispute, revenge, etc.). Random wars without cause are prohibited.

3

War Cooldown

After declaring war, there's a 48-hour (real time) cooldown before your gang can declare another war.

4

Admin Approval

All war declarations must be approved by server admins through a proper request system.

5

No Third-Party Involvement

Neutral parties cannot interfere in gang wars unless directly allied with one side.

6

War Duration

Wars last maximum 7 days. If unresolved, admins will mediate a conclusion.

7

Friendly Fire

Friendly fire is strictly prohibited. You are not allowed to harm teammates under any circumstances.

8

Safe Zones

No combat in spawn areas or designated safe zones, even during wars.

9

Surrender Terms

Clear surrender terms must be established before war begins (tributes, territory, etc.).

10

Leaving the Gang

If you are playing as a member of the gang, you must return all gang-related inventory when leaving. This includes weapons, and 15k money for leaving gang and other gang-owned items. The decision can only be made by the gang leader.
